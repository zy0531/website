I'm a PhD student in Department of Computer Science at Vanderbilt University. I'm advised by Dr. Bobby Bodenheimer. In my research, I quantify human's spatial perception and cognition in virtual and augmented environments to better understand the quality of the virtual contents and how it affects the interaction of people with immersive technology. My research lies at the intersection of virtual/augmented reality, human-computer interaction and spatial cognition.
